Modernizing the EDA toolchain.

Standing on the shoulders of giants.

The open-source community has spent decades building a remarkable set of capabilities for the electronic design automation marketplace — simulators, parsers, formats, verification frameworks, and tooling that millions of engineers rely on every day.

Ferrite Engineering's mission is to build on that foundation: to take what the community has created and turn it into commercial-grade products with the integration, polish, and modern user interfaces that engineering teams expect — and to give back to the community that made it all possible.

Our goal is a suite of EDA products serving the segment of the market that needs integration and modern user experiences, with strong capabilities, priced affordably relative to the established commercial tools.

What we're building

Modern, cross-platform EDA tools with open-source cores and commercial enhancements.

In development

Our first product

Our first product is in active development — a modern, cross-platform tool built on open-source foundations, with a polished, integrated experience and pricing that respects the budget of the engineers it's built for. More details soon.

More to come

Additional EDA tools are in early planning. The thread running through everything we build: open-core foundations, modern UX, affordable pricing, and a strong line of credit back to the open-source community we depend on.
